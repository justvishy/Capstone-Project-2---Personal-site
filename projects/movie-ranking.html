The Best Movies According to Vishy

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Imaginary

A woman returns to her childhood home to discover that the imaginary friend she left behind is very real and unhappy that she abandoned him.

The Family Plan

A former top assassin living incognito as a suburban dad must take his unsuspecting family on the run when his past catches up to him.

Scream VI

In the next installment, the survivors of the Ghostface killings leave Woodsboro behind and start a fresh chapter in New York City.
